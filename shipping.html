Our Delivery System

Getting ready for a shipment

courier companies and booking platforms allow shipping various items. Nevertheless, almost every courier service provider is specialized in different types of packages. It means that one company could be offering shipping services up to 30kg, whereas others accept to ship heavier shipment without any problems. That’s why it’s important to know the dimensions and the weight of what you wish to send and then find the right courier for it. This is not only to obtain an accurate price but also to make sure your package fits the standards of the selected courier company. Whenever the package is non-standard, the driver might refuse to collect it. Since courier drivers are not equipped with any measuring devices, they might still pick up the package, but it could be refused in a depot later on.

Placing an order

So, you’ve already measured your package and explored your options, and now it’s time to place an order. It doesn’t matter if you decide to do that via booking platform or directly with the courier company; Similar rules apply.

When it comes to your address, don’t hesitate to provide the courier company as many details as possible. Using door-to-door services allows you to order a pick-up from almost any address. The driver has just to be able to find the place easily. Don’t forget about additional information that could be needed, such some access codes to the property or the names written on the doorbell (if it’s different than yours). Simply add anything that you think might help the driver locate your flat.
